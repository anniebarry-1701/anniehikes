I solo-hiked 800 miles on the Pacific Crest Trail from April - June 2017.

My Story

I started at the US-Mexico Border in Campo, California and ended at Kearsarge Pass, which is parallel to Fresno, California. I currently plan to finish the remaining 1800 miles of the PCT via weeklong trips over the next few years to come.

How it Works

  1. Start at any PCT trailhead or junction.
  2. Hike ~100 miles, and sleep outside 5-6 nights.
  3. Hop off at any PCT trailhead or junction. Hitchhike into the closest town. Resupply food at a grocery store or at the local post office (meal-mailing prep ahead required).
repeat!
